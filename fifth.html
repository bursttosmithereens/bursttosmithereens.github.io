Placid

Schedule Maker for Optimum Productivity

Project placid is being designed to serve as a smart scheduler. Instead of making lists like many of the scheduling apps and calenders availale in the market allow the user to do, placid makes the experience personalised. Based upon custom requirements, the ML engine of the web app creates daily goals to optimize performance. The app is designed to maximize user user satisifaction to optimize productivity in the long run.
