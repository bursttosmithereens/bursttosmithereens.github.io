About

Meraki lab is a sole proprietorship company established in the summer of 2020. The goal of the lab is to use artificial intelligence to produce creative technical solutions. We work with technologies such as computer vision, reinforcement learning, recommender systems, and the internet of things to craft our products. We currently deal with original, collaborative, and contract-based projects. To learn more about our work and discuss potential collaborations, please refer to our contact page or email us at contact.merakilab@gmail.com.

Featured Projects

Cybernation:
An online learning portal

The cybernation project is a web application developed purely using Django. The portal serves as a one stop destination for learning cyber security principles. It comes coupled with an interface to help understand how real-life hacks take place.

PINACC:
A security provision

PINACC was designed during an IEEE affiliate ideathon. The device is a self-defense tool disguised as a hairpin. The project was published in the gazette of the Intellectual Property Department of India in November 2020 and is presently in the process of being patented.

HARP: Real-time Anxiety Alleviation

HARP proposes the delivery of Cognitive Behaviour Therapy to patients through a combination of BCI machines and reinforcement learning. The idea is to maximize user engagement to alter thought patterns and facillitate the reduction of anxiety levels. Our goal is to develop cheaper alternatives to BCI hardware, an open-source GUI for real-time EEG visualization, and an RL-driven game.

  • Learn More

UnClog: Urban flooding mitigation

UnClog is designed to solve the urban flooding problem caused due to uneven roads. The project uses dedicated drones and convolution neural networks to detect potholes and unevenness of roads. The result is a minimal intervention system that produces a list of flooding prone areas. UnClog is now patented under IP Australia.
