Maskavior

Smart Face Covering With Real-Time Alert System

Maskavior is a safety device for workers and employees working into a chemical related industries. Our primary purpose is to supply safety equipment. The entire project is built on the Internet of Things and is made up of hardware components, the majority of which are sensors. We intend to provide a less priced yet high-quality alternative to the pricey gear that many small firms cannot afford.
