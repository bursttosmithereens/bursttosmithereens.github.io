stcs

Smart Traffic Control System

STCS strives to help solve smooth traffic systems so that in the event of an emergency, emergency vehicles (Ambulance or Fire Brigade) can have a clear path on the road so that they can get at their destination on time and accidents can be avoided. The entire system is built on the Internet of Things and a server that will store the data and assist in signal exchange.
