Usher

Assisted Travel for the Visually Challenged

Usher's idea aims to assist vision impaired persons who experience daily challenges while travelling and walking on the roadside. A hardware board, a few sensors, an input processing machine learning module, and a camera are all part of it. The gadget will send a constant message to a blind person as they are going along the street via a hearing device.
